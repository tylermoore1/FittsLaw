Our Experiment

When people are using a computer, they tend to use the trackpad instead of an actual mouse when they are pointing to stuff on the screen. So our thought process was to do an experiment to see if the index finger or middle finger was more efficient at moving across the screen. So for the actual experiment, we had two people test out their index and middle fingers. First, both people tested out their index finger at the same time and did multiple trials. Then we averaged the results to find the average speed for people using their index finger. After that, we did the same approach for two people using their middle finger.
